# Hackathon Demo Script (5 Minutes)

**Goal**: Impress the judges by demonstrating the complete end-to-end flow from unstructured document to proactive intelligent insight, proving the system is a cohesive "Brain" and not just a wrapper.

## Minute 0:00 - The Problem
- **Presenter**: "Industrial plants run on decades of unstructured PDFs and tribal knowledge. Today, we show you SureFlow Industrial OS—a multi-agent brain that turns dead files into a living, proactive intelligence."

## Minute 1:00 - Document Ingestion (The Document Agent)
- **Action**: Drag and drop a scanned "Centrifugal Pump P-101 OEM Manual" and a "Maintenance Log CSV" into the UI.
- **Visual**: The Temporal workflow progress bar lights up. You see the `Document Intelligence Agent` extracting text, while the `Knowledge Graph Agent` extracts the entity "P-101".
- **Presenter**: "Behind the scenes, our agents aren't just saving text. They are reading the layout, extracting the metadata, and wiring it into our Industrial Knowledge Graph."

## Minute 2:30 - The Copilot & Hybrid Search
- **Action**: Open the Industrial Copilot chat.
- **Prompt**: *"What is the standard operating pressure for P-101, and who worked on it last?"*
- **Visual**: The Copilot responds instantly. It cites the OEM Manual (RAG) for the pressure, and cites the graph for the last mechanic (John Doe).
- **Presenter**: "Notice the Hybrid Search. The agent reasoned across unstructured vector data and structured graph relationships simultaneously."

## Minute 3:30 - Maintenance & Lessons Learned Intelligence
- **Action**: Simulate a new Incident Report being filed for P-101 (Vibration threshold exceeded).
- **Visual**: The UI flashes. The `Lessons Learned Agent` intercepts the incident, reads the graph, and updates the Company Brain's **Reflection Memory**.
- **Action**: Ask the Copilot to generate a work order for a *different* pump, P-102.
- **Visual**: The Copilot generates it, but adds a high-priority warning: *"WARNING: Based on a recent incident with P-101 (same model), ensure bearing lubrication is checked before startup."*
- **Presenter**: "This is the true power of an Agentic OS. The system learned from a failure on one asset, updated its core memory, and proactively prevented the same mistake on a different asset."

## Minute 4:30 - Compliance & Graph Visualization
- **Action**: Open the Knowledge Graph Explorer tab.
- **Visual**: A beautiful React Flow node map. Click on `P-101`, watch it expand to show connected `Incidents`, `Manuals`, and `OSHA Regulations`. 
- **Presenter**: "SureFlow OS isn't a chatbot. It's an Industrial Brain." (End)
